Personal instructor consultation

Our personal instructors, highly qualified specialists who studied to the smallest detail the features of human anatomy and physiology, as well as psychology, medicine and dietetics. Due to this, they are able to develop a training process scheme (adequately selected load and fitness diet), with the achievement of intermediate goals, individually for each person, taking into considaration all its features.

We are ready to help you get comfortable in this difficult proces.

Do not experiment on yourself and your health - work with professionals!

Personal instructor services

Basic consultation

Basic consultation is carried out after providing the completed questionnaire. At the consultation, the instructor will provide a professional assessment and give recommendations on how to achieve this goal.

Duration:
up to 30 minutes
Price of consultation:
free
Personal consultation

Personal consultation is carried out after providing the completed questionnaire and includes a detailed analysis of training schemes, diet and taking sports supplements, an analysis of the training diary is conducted, on the basis of which the instructor will provide recommendations if necessary

Duration:
up to 60 minutes
Price of consultation:
$20
Wellness rehabilitation

Consultation is carried out after submitting the completed application form. This area involves counseling or an individual lesson with the instructor, on the prevention of diseases and the restoration of the spine, joints, various muscles of the body, rehabilitation after injuries and operations.

Duration:
up to 60 minutes
Price of consultation:
$10
